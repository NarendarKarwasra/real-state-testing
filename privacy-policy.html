Privacy Policy

At BRICKKLY, we value the trust you place in us and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect the personal information of users who visit our website or use any of our services. This policy applies to all interactions you have with BRICKKLY through our website, mobile interface, or any communication channel where your data is shared with us.

When you browse our website or contact us through forms, WhatsApp, email, or phone, we may collect personal information such as your full name, contact number, email address, city of residence, and preferences related to property type, budget, and location. Additionally, we may collect non-identifiable information like your IP address, device type, browser details, location data, pages visited, time spent on each page, and how you interact with our content. This information may be gathered using cookies, pixel tags, analytics tools like Google Analytics, and other tracking technologies that help us understand user behavior and improve our digital experience.

We use the personal information we collect primarily to respond to your queries, suggest suitable real estate projects based on your interests, schedule site visits, offer investment guidance, and provide follow-up support as needed. Additionally, the data helps us analyze user patterns, improve our website’s performance, create marketing campaigns tailored to user interests, and ensure that our services remain efficient and relevant. If you opt in to receive marketing updates from BRICKKLY, we may use your contact information to send you property alerts, newsletters, offers, and project-related updates. You may opt out of such communication at any time by contacting us directly.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party for commercial gain. However, we may share your data with trusted partners such as developers, legal advisors, or channel agents — but only in cases where it is necessary to fulfill your property inquiry or with your explicit consent. In rare cases, we may be legally required to disclose your information to regulatory authorities or in response to legal proceedings, subpoenas, or court orders.

At BRICKKLY, we take reasonable and industry-standard precautions to ensure the security and confidentiality of your personal data. Our systems are designed with appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration of your data. However, no method of online data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You retain full control over your personal data shared with us. You may at any time request access to your data, ask for corrections or updates, or request deletion of your information from our systems. If you wish to make any such request, please email us at info@brickkly.com and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by applicable law. If you stop engaging with us, your data will be archived or deleted as per our retention policy, unless legally required to keep it for a longer period.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, developer sites, or payment gateways. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy policies or data handling practices of these third-party entities. We encourage you to review their privacy terms before engaging with them.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our business practices, technologies, or applicable laws. We will post all updates on this page and revise the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to revisit this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
